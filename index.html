CSP (Charity of the Soaring Phoenix), an establishing nonprofit development and humanitarian organization devoted to protecting human dignity and fighting poverty, was founded in 2021. By enhancing communities' and households' ability through social, political, and economic possibilities, providing emergency relief, influencing policy choices, and addressing prejudice, CSP promotes long-lasting change. For the past two years, CSP has shown the road to a better life for the most disadvantaged people in the globe in ten locations and for 7,000 individuals annually.
